About us



Inheritance

In 2015, Hoa Phat Furniture marked a development step in both scale and stature with the investment in a series of production factories across the country. The company's business strategy is adapted and flexibly changed with each stage, ensuring the coverage of the entire market with all products in the development stage when the company turns 20 years old.

Achievement

With over 2,000 employees and 4 branches across the country, Hoa Phat Furniture always focuses on investing in a key, solid and effective strategy. With modern equipment from Japan, Italy, Germany, and Taiwan, the Company's production lines are considered as the most modern furniture production lines in Vietnam at present. In which export activities. Hoa Phat furniture products have appeared in the markets of Japan, Singapore, Laos, Cambodia, the Middle East, Ukraine and are promoting exports to major markets such as the EU, Eastern Europe, and the US.
